About Giada Graziano

Founder, Coach, Author, Podcast Host

From Small Town Dreams to Global Impact

Giada's journey began in a small town in Southern Italy, where fashion seemed like a distant dream. Through determination and strategic planning, she transformed her passion into a thriving career that has impacted thousands worldwide.

Her breakthrough came with her first internship at Alexander McQueen, where she gained invaluable insights into the luxury fashion world. While still studying, she launched Glam Observer, a platform that would become the go-to resource for fashion career guidance.

Today, Giada has helped thousands of aspiring professionals land coveted positions at prestigious brands like Dior, Vogue, Gucci, and Marie Claire, proving that with the right guidance, anyone can break into fashion.

Vision & Mission

Giada's mission is crystal clear: to make fashion careers accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background. She believes that talent and passion should never be limited by geography or circumstances.
