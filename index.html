About Giada Graziano

Giada Graziano is a fashion career expert, educator, and the founder of Glam Observer. With over a decade of experience working with top luxury brands in Milan, Paris, and New York, she's dedicated to helping aspiring fashion professionals break into the industry.

Through her courses, books, and podcast, Giada has helped thousands of students worldwide land their dream jobs at companies like Chanel, Vogue, and Gucci.
